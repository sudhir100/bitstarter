Welcome to SimpliTask, your one-stop task management solution.

SimpliTask keeps track of all the items in your todo list and intelligently categorizes them. Based on what sort of activity you have noted, SimpliTask will send reminders, and also provide context-sensitive information for that specific activity. Gone are the days when you have to do several steps to achieve something simple. SimpliTask will do the needful, so that when things need to get done, you just have to do it - all the details are provided by SimpliTask.

This is the one tool that you cannot do without.

Creating tasks in SimpliTask is easy. Just specify what type of task you want - you could either Do something, or you could Buy something, or you could Meet someone. You then specify the actual task to be completed. You also specify the date when you'd like to perform the task. Thats it - you're done. Based on the type of task SimpliTask will provide you all the relevant information at the appropriate time. Reminders and other behavior can be configured to provide a truly custom experience.

Let's say you want to buy an iPad on a specific date. You create the task as previously described. Once that date arrives (or earlier based on what you have chosen), SimpliTask will send you a reminder to buy the item. In addition it will provide stores where the item can be purchased and at what price. For online stores, it will show the store name and the price and a Buy button which wil take you to that item in the store for purchase. For local stores it will still show the store name and price and in addition the details will show you the store information(location, hours etc.) and a map with directions of how to get there.

Now let's say you have an appointment to meet somebody at a specific spot on a specifc date/time. A little before that time SimpliTask will provide you a reminder as well as provide you with a map with specific directions to meet that person.

FAQ

How is SimpliTask different from other to-do list apps?
Normal to-do list apps just provide a way for users to list their tasks and get reminders when they are due. SimpliTask is context-sensitive and provides you with all the information needed at the right time to successfully acthive the task at hand.
What contexts are supported by SimpliTask?
At present SimpliTask supports purchases, meetings and normal to-do items. We are looking at other aspects to further expand this in the future.
